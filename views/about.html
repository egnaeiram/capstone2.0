About Ryde is a bicycle sharing platform that provides a convenient and eco-friendly mode of transportation for Montreal urban residents and tourists.

The company offers a variety of bicycles for rent that can be picked up and dropped off at various locations around the city.

Users can access the bikes through our Ryde app, which allows them to locate and reserve bikes, track their ride, and make payments. Our app is available on AppStore and GoogleStore.

Ryde aims to promote sustainable transportation and reduce traffic congestion, making cities like Montreal more livable and environmentally friendly.
